Math Problems

Here are some math problems:

1. Amy had 13757 cupcakes. Jack had 58385. How many cupcakes do they have altogether? Use 2 strategies to solve the problem.

2. Cash wants to give 185 fidget spinners.
Each person gets 5 fidget spinners.
How many people did Cash give fidget spinners to? 

3. There are 125 people in a restaurant.
Each person has 5 pieces of food.
How many pieces of food are there altogether? 

4. Michel had 1923 donuts. James had 3752
donuts. How many more donuts does James have than Michel?
